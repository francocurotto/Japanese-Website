### TE-form of I-adjectives
- Rule: Change I with KUTE
- Example:
    - YASUI → YASUKUTE (inexpensive)
- Exception:
    - II → YOKUTE (glad)

### TE-from of NA-adjectives
- Rule: Add DE at the end
- Example:
    - GENKI → GENKI DE (healthy)
